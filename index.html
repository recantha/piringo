PiRingo internet-of-things Christmas decoration

Welcome to the Horne house. From here you can send instructions to my Raspberry Pi and PiRingo. Different patterns are available and you should be able to see the lights being triggered via the streaming video.

Please note: the camera needs to be suspended above the board but I haven't quite figured out how to do this yet! Because of that, it's very close to the LEDs and is therefore quite blurry!
To find out how I did this, visit my blog Raspberry PiPod. The post on the PiRingo is here and the post on streaming video is here.
